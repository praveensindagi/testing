    Government
    Partnerships

    We provide strategic and technical advisory services to strengthen monitoring and evaluation practices and strategies.

    Government partnerships lead to a varied and customised approach to capacity building. Our multifaceted approach includes, but is not limited to: technical advisory support, evidence-based insights, custom training and workshops, policy dialogues, and knowledge sharing events.

    Read about some of our key partnerships with Governments across Asia.

    Government of Tamil Nadu

    The Government of Tamil Nadu (GoTN) has partnered with CLEAR/J-PAL South Asia since 2014. CLEAR South Asia’s capacity building and advisory activities with GoTN spans various thematic areas such data analysis, data use, and the creation of evaluation tools and guidelines. We have conducted customised workshops and training sessions for GoTN staff from various departments, such as Evaluation & Applied Research, Economics & Statistics, and Social Welfare. We have also worked with trainees of the Indian Administrative Service in Tamil Nadu.
