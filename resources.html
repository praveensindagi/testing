    Resources

    We provide strategic and technical advisory services to strengthen monitoring and evaluation practices and strategies.

    To collate and share the knowledge we gain through our work in monitoring and evaluation, the CLEAR South Asia team develops Knowledge Products in the form of toolkits, reference guides, or concept documents. These resources are developed as public goods and can be useful for various stakeholders in the M&E space including evaluators, implementers, policy-makers, students, and researchers.

    Ultimately, these knowledge products are designed to help improve the understanding of M&E concepts and encourage the adoption of an evidence-based approach to decision making. For those who already recognise the need for evidence generation and use, these products serve as practical guides on how to operationalise evaluations, generate rigorous evidence, and use this information to make key policy decisions.
